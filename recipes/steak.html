Steak

Description

The perfect steak starts with the right cut. But trying to pick out a steak at your grocery store's butcher counter can be all sorts of intimidating. Let's change that.

Here you'll learn about eight different types of steak cuts and how to cook them correctly.

Ingredients

  • 4 (1/2 pound) beef cube steaks

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, divided

  • 2 teaspoons baking powder

  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

  • ¾ teaspoon salt

  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk

Steps

  1. Pound the Meat

  2. Make the Coating

  3. Dredge and Fry

  4. Make the Gravy
